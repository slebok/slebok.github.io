PV1 ⊧ EntropyEstimationcomputes the Shannon entropy for a given program

Application domain/field

  • Quantified information flow
  • Confidentiality measurements
  • Information leakage

Expected input

Boolean formula

Format:

CNF file

Expected output

Shannon entropy estimation

Internals

Estimates the Shannon entropy of a formula φ such that the computed estimate is guaranteed to lie with (1+−ϵ)-factor of the ground truth with confidence at least 1−δ. They focus on Boolean formulas that capture the relationship between input X and output Y of a given program. They estimate the Shannon entropy for this Boolean formula. Uses GANAK for model counting queries and SPUR for sampling queries.
