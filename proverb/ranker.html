PV1 ⊧ Rankerconstructs the complement of a Büchi automaton

Application domain/field

  • Büchi automata
  • Automata theory
  • Complementation

Expected input

Büchi automata

Format:

HOA or ba format

Expected output

Complement of the Büchi automaton

Internals

Ranker supports several types of ω-automata:
  • Büchi automata with mixed transition-based/state-based acceptance condition
  • Generalized co-Büchi automata
