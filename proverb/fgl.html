Type of tool

Rewriter/Library for ACL2

Internals

FGL is described as a term rewriter that transforms expressions acting on fixed-size data into Boolean formulas. One can then use equivalence checking between two sets of Boolean formulas to show that the implementation result matches the specification. FGL is integrated into ACL2.
