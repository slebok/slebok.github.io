PV0 tools in ProVerB

PV0 tools manipulate given and produced software artefacts with some degree of rigour. Conceptually such artefacts then may correspond to mathematical and formal entities, but this correspondence is mostly a matter of expectations. This category should be reasonably unpopulated since we mostly avoid to include such tools here.

  • Alloy Analyzer
  • ANTLR (works with grammars which are relatively strictly structured)
  • Apfloat (operates on extreme-precision real numbers (with millions of digits))
