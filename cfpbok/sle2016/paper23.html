SLE 2016 paper:
Xdiagram: A Declarative Textual DSL for Describing Diagram Editors (Tool Demo)

Andre Luís de Medeiros Santos, Eduardo Gomes

  • DOI: 10.1145/2997364.2997377
  • T3C: DSLs
    • Xdiagram is a DSL for specifying diagram editors for DSMLs.
  • T4A: Workbenches
    • It is meant to generate/support tooling (diagram editors), i.e., meta-tooling for language workbenches.
  • T4F: Visualisation
    • Its purpose is to define and realize graphical syntax/editors, directly tied to visualization.
  • T1A: Design
    • It supports language design by enabling definition of concrete (diagrammatic) notations.
