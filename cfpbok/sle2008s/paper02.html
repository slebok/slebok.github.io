SLE 2008 SI paper:
The “Physics” of Notations: Toward a Scientific Basis for Constructing Visual Notations in Software Engineering

Daniel Laurence Moody

  • DOI: 10.1109/TSE.2009.67
  • Extended version of Evaluating the Visual Syntax of UML: An Analysis of the Cognitive Effectiveness of the UML Family of Diagrams (SLE 2008)
  • T1A: Design
    • The paper develops a principled design theory (principles and rationale) for constructing visual modelling notations, i.e., language design for visual syntax.
  • T5H: Usability
    • The proposed principles aim at cognitively effective notations and improved human comprehensibility, making usability a central evaluation dimension.
  • T4F: Visualisation
    • It focuses on the visual (diagrammatic) representation of language constructs and how they should be visualised for effective communication.
