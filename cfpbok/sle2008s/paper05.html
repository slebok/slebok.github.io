SLE 2008 SI paper:
A Model-Based Approach to Families of Embedded Domain-Specific Languages

Jesús Sánchez Cuadrado, Jesús Garćıa Molina

  • DOI: 10.1109/TSE.2009.14
  • T1E: Reuse
    • The main contribution is a reuse-oriented approach to build families of embedded DSLs via systematic composition mechanisms.
  • T2C: Variability
    • It explicitly treats “families of DSLs” and variability through composing and configuring multiple DSL definitions.
  • T1D: Composition
    • The approach centres on composition of abstract syntax, concrete syntax, and transformations, i.e., modular combination of language artefacts.
  • T3C: DSLs
    • Embedded DSLs are the target language form and the paper contributes a framework and case study for engineering them.
