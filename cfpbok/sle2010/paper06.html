SLE 2010 paper:
Feature and Meta-Models in Clafer: Mixed, Specialized, and Coupled

Kacper Bąk, Krzysztof Czarnecki, Andrzej Wąsowski

  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-642-19440-5_7
  • T2C: Variability
    • It targets variability and feature modeling, including how features relate to (and can be coupled with) meta-models.
  • T3C: DSLs
    • Clafer is presented as a specialized language to express feature/meta-model structures concisely.
  • T1D: Composition
    • A major point is the integration/coupling of feature models and meta-models within one notation.
