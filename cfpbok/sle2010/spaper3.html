SLE 2010 short paper:
DSLTrans: A Turing Incomplete Transformation Language

Bruno Barroca, Levi Lúcio, Vasco Amaral, Roberto Félix, Vasco Sousa

  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-642-19440-5_19
  • T3B: Transformation Languages
    • DSLTrans is explicitly a transformation language, designed to express model transformations.
  • T5D: Formal Methods
    • Turing incompleteness is used to guarantee properties like termination and confluence, enabling verification by construction.
  • T4B: Horizontal Transformation
    • Its constructs target systematic horizontal model mappings with traceable links and layered rules.
