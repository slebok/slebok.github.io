SLE 2010 paper:
JTL: A Bidirectional and Change Propagating Transformation Language

Antonio Cicchetti, Davide Di Ruscio, Romina Eramo, Alfonso Pierantonio

  • 🏆 Most Influencial Paper Award at SLE 2020
  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-642-19440-5_11
  • T3B: Transformation Languages
    • JTL is introduced as a dedicated transformation language for bidirectional, change-propagating model transformations.
  • T4B: Horizontal Transformation
    • The language targets model-to-model mapping/synchronisation and supports change propagation between related models.
  • T2B: Evolution
    • Bidirectionality and change propagation are motivated by keeping evolving models consistent across edits.
