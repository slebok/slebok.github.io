SLE 2010 paper:
Automated Co-evolution of GMF Editor Models

Davide Di Ruscio, Ralf Lämmel, Alfonso Pierantonio

  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-642-19440-5_9
  • T2B: Evolution
    • The main contribution is automated co-evolution of models when GMF-based editor/model artifacts change.
  • T4B: Horizontal Transformation
    • Co-evolution is realised via model-to-model transformations/migrations across versions.
  • T4A: Workbenches
    • The context and automation are tightly connected to the GMF/Eclipse tooling stack.
