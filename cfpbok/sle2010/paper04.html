SLE 2010 paper:
Metamodel Usage Analysis for Identifying Metamodel Improvements

Markus Herrmannsdörfer, Daniel Ratiu, Maximilian Koegel

  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-642-19440-5_5
  • T5B: Language Description Analysis
    • The work proposes analyses over metamodels and conforming models to detect mismatches and improvement opportunities.
  • T2B: Evolution
    • The analyses are used to drive metamodel/language evolution via refactorings, constructors, and destructors.
  • T5E: Empirical Evaluation
    • It includes an empirical study over multiple metamodels and a corpus of models to validate the approach.
