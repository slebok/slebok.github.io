SLE 2010 paper:
Subjective-C: Bringing Context to Mobile Platform Programming

Sebastián González, Nicolás Cardozo, Kim Mens, Alfredo Cádiz, Jean-Christophe Libbrecht, Julien Goffaux

  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-642-19440-5_15
  • T1A: Design
    • It introduces a language/design extension (Subjective-C) to incorporate context into mobile programming.
  • T3C: DSLs
    • The extension is tailored to the domain of context-aware mobile applications, making it DSL-like in intent.
  • T1D: Composition
    • A key issue is integrating the language constructs with an existing mobile platform and its APIs.
