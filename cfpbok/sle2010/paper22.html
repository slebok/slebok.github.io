SLE 2010 paper:
Extending DMM Behavior Specifications for Visual Execution and Debugging

Nils Bandener, Christian Soltenborn, Gregor Engels

  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-642-19440-5_24
  • T4F: Visualisation
    • The work emphasizes visual execution and debugging, i.e., visualization of runtime behavior and debugging feedback.
  • T1C: Behavioural Semantics
    • It builds on behavioral/executable specifications of DMM models to enable execution and debugging.
  • T4A: Workbenches
    • The contribution includes tool support to execute and debug models in a visual manner.
