SLE 2017 paper:
Towards a taxonomy of grammar smells

Mats Stijlaart, Vadim Zaytsev

  • DOI: 10.1145/3136014.3136035
  • T5B: Language Description Analysis
    • It proposes a taxonomy of grammar smells, i.e., systematic analysis of grammar/language descriptions.
  • T2B: Evolution
    • Grammar smells are motivated by grammar refactoring and maintenance of evolving language definitions.
  • T3A: Meta-languages
    • The objects analyzed are grammars, which are meta-language artifacts used to define syntax.
  • T4A: Workbenches
    • A taxonomy supports building analysis/refactoring tools for grammars, which the paper discusses as a motivation.
