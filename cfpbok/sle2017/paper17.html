SLE 2017 paper:
Robust Projectional Editing

Friedrich Steimann, Marcus Frenkel, Markus Völter

  • DOI: 10.1145/3136014.3136034
  • T4A: Workbenches
    • The paper addresses projectional editor technology and how to make projectional editing more robust.
  • T1B: Static Semantics
    • It emphasises enforcing well-formedness rules (static constraints) in addition to metamodel conformance.
  • T5H: Usability
    • A key goal is improving user experience by preventing/handling ill-formed intermediate states during editing.
  • T1D: Composition
    • It discusses how editing constraints interact with composed language fragments and their well-formedness rules.
