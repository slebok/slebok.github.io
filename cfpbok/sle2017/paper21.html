SLE 2017 paper:
Structural model subtyping with OCL constraints

Artur Boronat

  • DOI: 10.1145/3136014.3136026
  • T1B: Static Semantics
    • The main contribution is a static semantics notion of structural model subtyping constrained by OCL.
  • T5D: Formal Methods
    • It develops formal conditions/definitions for subtyping and constraint satisfaction.
  • T1E: Reuse
    • Subtyping is motivated by enabling flexible reuse of models and transformations across related model types.
  • T3C: DSLs
    • The setting is model-driven engineering where models and constraints form a domain-specific modeling language.
