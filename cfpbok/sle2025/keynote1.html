SLE 2025 keynote:
A New DSL Textbook in Town!

Thorsten Berger

  • DOI: 10.1145/3732771.3742707
  • T3C: DSLs
    • The keynote focuses on domain-specific languages (DSLs) and how they raise the level of abstraction for automation in software projects.
  • T2E: Documentation
    • It centers on a DSL textbook and teaching material, making documentation/knowledge transfer a core artifact.
  • T6A: Experience Reports
    • The talk reports practical experiences from a decade of teaching DSL engineering, in the style of an experience report.
