SLE 2025 paper:
Detecting Resource Leaks on Android with Alpakka

Gustavo Soares Santos, João Bispo, Alexandra Mendes

  • DOI: 10.1145/3732771.3742724
  • T4B: Horizontal Transformation
    • Alpakka is a source-to-source compiler over Smali that transforms programs to detect and repair resource leaks.
  • T2B: Evolution
    • Automatically localizing and fixing resource leaks supports maintenance and regression prevention in Android apps.
  • T5E: Empirical Evaluation
    • It reports an empirical evaluation on a dataset of real APKs and quantifies findings such as false positive rates.
