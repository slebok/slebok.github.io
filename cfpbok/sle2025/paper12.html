SLE 2025 paper:
Optimize Effect Handling for Tail-resumption with Stack Unwinding

Yuze Fu, Shigeru Chiba

  • DOI: 10.1145/3732771.3742721
  • T4D: Interpretation
    • Effect handlers are a runtime control-flow mechanism, and the paper proposes a concrete runtime implementation strategy.
  • T5F: Performance
    • The goal is to reduce overhead for tail-resumptive handlers, with performance as the main evaluation dimension.
  • T1C: Behavioural Semantics
    • The work is about executable semantics of algebraic effects/handlers and how they are realized operationally.
