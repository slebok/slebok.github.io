Can Programming Be Liberated from the Functional Style?

Friedrich Steimann

  • DOI: 10.1145/3732771.3742708
  • T3D: GPLs
    • It discusses general-purpose programming paradigms (functional vs. relational/modelling styles) rather than a specific DSL.
  • T1A: Design
    • The argument is about (re-)designing programming languages around different core abstractions and composition of results.
  • T1C: Behavioural Semantics
    • It reasons about computation/meaning (what programs can produce) at the level of behavioural/executable semantics.
