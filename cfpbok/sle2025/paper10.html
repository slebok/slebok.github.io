SLE 2025 paper:
Integrating Model Checking into a Live Modeling Environment

Joeri Exelmans, Ciprian Teodorov, Hans Vangheluwe

  • DOI: 10.1145/3732771.3742718
  • T5D: Formal Methods
    • Model checking is a formal-methods technique, and integrating it is the paper’s primary contribution.
  • T4D: Interpretation
    • The setting is live modeling where models execute under an interpreter and are edited at runtime.
  • T4E: Simulation
    • Witness traces/counterexamples are produced as integrated execution scenarios, aligning with simulation-style exploration of behaviours.
