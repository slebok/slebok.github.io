SLE 2025 paper:
Exploratory, Omniscient, and Multiverse Diagnostics in Debuggers for Non-Deterministic Languages

Damian Frölich, Tommaso Pacciani, L. Thomas van Binsbergen

  • DOI: 10.1145/3732771.3742719
  • T4D: Interpretation
    • The contribution is debugging support for non-deterministic language runtimes via multiverse state-space exploration.
  • T5C: Testing
    • It generalizes breakpoint-like mechanisms and exploration logics that support systematic debugging/testing of behaviours.
  • T1C: Behavioural Semantics
    • Non-deterministic execution semantics are central, since the debugger explores alternative execution paths and resulting states.
