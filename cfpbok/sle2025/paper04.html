SLE 2025 paper:
Boosting Parallel Parsing through Cyclic Operator Precedence Grammars

Michele Chiari, Michele Giornetta, Dino Mandrioli, Matteo Pradella

  • DOI: 10.1145/3732771.3742712
  • T4C: Vertical Transformation
    • It develops a (parallel) parser generator for operator-precedence grammars, a compilation front-end concern.
  • T5F: Performance
    • Parallel parsing is motivated and evaluated primarily in terms of performance and scalability improvements.
  • T3A: Meta-languages
    • The approach is grounded in formal grammar formalisms (OPL) used as meta-languages for syntax definition.
