SLE 2023 tool/demo paper:
A Tool for the Definition and Deployment of Platform-Independent Bots on Open Source Projects

Adem Ait, Javier Luis Cánovas Izquierdo, Jordi Cabot

  • DOI: 10.1145/3623476.3623524
  • T2D: Deployment
    • The paper focuses on defining and deploying bots in a platform-independent way across open-source projects.
  • T4A: Workbenches
    • The contribution is realized as a toolchain for specifying bots and deploying them to target repositories.
  • T3C: DSLs
    • The bot definitions resemble a domain-specific specification language for project automation tasks.
