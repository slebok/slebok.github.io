SLE 2023 paper:
A Low-Code Platform for Systematic Component-Oriented Language Composition

Jérôme Pfeiffer, Andreas Wortmann

  • DOI: 10.1145/3623476.3623516
  • T4A: Workbenches
    • The main contribution is a low-code platform (tool) that supports systematic component-oriented language composition.
  • T1D: Composition
    • The platform operationalizes language composition mechanisms by coordinating multiple language constituents (syntax and semantics).
  • T4F: Visualisation
    • The approach relies on a graphical, web-based interface typical of visualization-oriented tooling.
