SLE 2023 paper:
Temporal Breakpoints for Multiverse Debugging

Matthias Pasquier, Ciprian Teodorov, Frédéric Jouault, Matthias Brun, Luka Le Roux, Loïc Lagadec

  • DOI: 10.1145/3623476.3623526
  • T5C: Testing
    • The paper extends multiverse debugging with temporal breakpoints, advancing debugging/testing support for exploring executions.
  • T4D: Interpretation
    • Breakpoints are defined over runtime execution traces of nondeterministic/concurrent programs.
  • T5G: Traceability
    • Temporal breakpoints rely on richer temporal properties over execution histories, strengthening trace-based reasoning.
