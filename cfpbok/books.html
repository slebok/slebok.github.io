Books

This list is admittedly short and possibly incomplete. If you think your book or a book you know, qualifies as an SLE book, please add it by emailing the maintainer of CfPBoK.

  • (2008) Model-Driven Software Development: Technology, Engineering, Management by Markus Völter
  • (2008) Software Language Engineering: Creating Domain-Specific Languages Using Metamodels by Anneke Kleppe

  • (2010) DSLs in Action by Debasish Ghosh

  • (2013) DSL Engineering: Designing, Implementing and Using Domain-Specific Languages by Markus Völter, Sebastian Benz, Christian Dietrich, Birgit Engelmann, Mats Helander, Lennart C. L. Kats, Eelco Visser, Guido Wachsmuth
  • (2013) Implementing Domain-Specific Languages with Xtext and Xtend (first edition) by Lorenzo Bettini
  • (2016) Implementing Domain-Specific Languages with Xtext and Xtend (second edition) by Lorenzo Bettini
  • (2017) Engineering Modeling Languages by Benoît Combemale, Robert B. France, Jean-Marc Jézéquel, Bernhard Rumpe, Jim Steel, Didier Vojtisek
  • (2017) Model-Driven Software Engineering in Practice, Second Edition by Marco Brambilla, Jordi Cabot, Manuel Wimmer
  • (2018) Software Languages: Syntax, Semantics, and Metaprogramming by Ralf Lämmel
  • (2021) MontiCore Language Workbench and Library Handbook by Bernhard Rumpe
  • (2021) Domain-Specific Languages in Practice by Antonio Bucchiarone, Antonio Cicchetti, Federico Ciccozzi, Alfonso Pierantonio
  • (2023) Domain-Specific Languages: Effective Modeling, Automation, and Reuse by Andrzej Wąsowski, Thorsten Berger
  • (2024) Building User-Friendly DSLs by Meinte Boersma
