SLE 2018 SI paper:
Purely functional GLL parsing

L. Thomas van Binsbergen, Elizabeth Scott, Adrian Johnstone

  • DOI: 10.1016/j.cola.2020.100945
  • Extended version of GLL parsing with flexible combinators (SLE 2018)
  • T4C: Vertical Transformation
    • The paper develops a generalised parsing procedure (FUN-GLL) and derives parser combinators, i.e., core front-end technology in the compilation pipeline.
  • T3A: Meta-languages
    • It provides an embedded “BNF combinator library” that effectively acts as a meta-language for writing executable syntax specifications with semantic actions.
  • T5F: Performance
    • It includes quantitative comparisons of alternative implementations, focusing on practical parsing overheads and efficiency trade-offs.
