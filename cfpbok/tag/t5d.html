Topic T5D: Formal Methods

Work applying formal verification and rigorous proof-based methods to software languages and software language artefacts: mechanised proofs, model checking, theorem proving, correctness of compilers, optimisations and transformations, formally verified semantics. Empirical performance benchmarking belongs to T5F.
