Topic T2B: Evolution

Language evolution, co-evolution, maintenance, migration. Work about software language change and its consequences: language evolution, coupled/co-evolution of languages with tools or language definitions with language instances, migrations, refactorings across versions, maintenance techniques for language ecosystems. Process aspects often get secondary T2A, tracking changes by linking related artefacts can be secondary T5G.
