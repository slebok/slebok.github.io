Topic T2C: Variability

Product lines and families. Work that treats families of software languages as first-class: variability modelling, software/language product lines, feature models for DSLs, configurable language components, systematic derivation of language variants. If variability is achieved primarily via modular composition mechanisms, T1D may be primary.
