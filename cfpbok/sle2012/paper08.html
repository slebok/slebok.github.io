SLE 2012 paper:
Model Transformation Co-evolution: A Semi-automatic Approach

Jokin García, Óscar Díaz, Maider Azanza

  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-642-36089-3_9
  • T2B: Evolution
    • The paper tackles maintaining transformations as metamodels evolve, i.e., co-evolution as a maintenance problem.
  • T3B: Transformation Languages
    • The artifacts being repaired and adapted are model transformations, with explicit methods for updating them.
  • T4A: Workbenches
    • The approach is semi-automatic, implying tool support for detecting/adapting broken transformations.
