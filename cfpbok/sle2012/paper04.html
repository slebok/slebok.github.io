SLE 2012 paper:
Metamodelling for Grammarware Researchers

Richard F. Paige, Dimitrios S. Kolovos, Fiona Polack

  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-642-36089-3_5
  • Original shorter version of A tutorial on metamodelling for grammar researchers (SLE 2012 SI)
  • T6D: Synergies
    • The paper’s explicit goal is to bridge “grammarware” and “modelware” communities by aligning concepts and practices.
  • T3A: Meta-languages
    • It reframes grammar-based language engineering artifacts in terms of meta-modeling concepts and notations.
  • T4A: Workbenches
    • It discusses tool-supported workflows (e.g., with EMF-style meta-models) that grammarware researchers can adopt.
