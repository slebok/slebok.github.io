SLE 2012 paper:
Island Grammar-Based Parsing Using GLL and Tom

Ali Afroozeh, Jean-Christophe Bach, Mark van den Brand, Adrian Johnstone, Maarten Manders, Pierre-Etienne Moreau, Elizabeth Scott

  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-642-36089-3_13
  • T4A: Workbenches
    • The core contribution is a parsing technique and its tool-level realization using generalized parsing (GLL) and Tom.
  • T1D: Composition
    • Island grammars are motivated by composing parsers with “holes” for embedded/unknown fragments, supporting language integration.
  • T3A: Meta-languages
    • The approach relies on grammar formalisms and rewriting infrastructure (Tom) to specify and implement the parsing strategy.
