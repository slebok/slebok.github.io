SLE 2012 paper:
Profile-Based Abstraction and Analysis of Attribute Grammar Evaluation

Anthony M. Sloane

  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-642-36089-3_3
  • Original shorter version of Domain-specific program profiling and its application to attribute grammars and term rewriting (SLE 2012 SI)
  • T5B: Language Description Analysis
    • The paper analyzes properties of attribute-grammar evaluation (driven by profiling) to support understanding and optimization.
  • T5F: Performance
    • Profiling-based abstractions are used to reason about evaluation costs and performance bottlenecks.
  • T3A: Meta-languages
    • Attribute grammars are the underlying language-definition formalism being studied and characterized.
