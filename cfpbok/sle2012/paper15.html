SLE 2012 paper:
Declarative Name Binding and Scope Rules

Gabriël Konat, Lennart C. L. Kats, Guido Wachsmuth, Eelco Visser

  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-642-36089-3_18
  • T1B: Static Semantics
    • Name binding and scoping are central static-semantics concerns, and the paper provides a compile-time resolution approach.
  • T3A: Meta-languages
    • It introduces a declarative meta-language for specifying name binding rules in reusable, language-parametric terms.
  • T4A: Workbenches
    • Integration into the Spoofax Language Workbench and support for editor services position it squarely as a tooling contribution.
