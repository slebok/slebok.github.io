SLE 2008 paper:
Evaluating the Visual Syntax of UML: An Analysis of the Cognitive Effectiveness of the UML Family of Diagrams

Daniel Laurence Moody, Jos van Hillegersberg

  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-642-00434-6_3
  • Original shorter version of The “Physics” of Notations: Toward a Scientific Basis for Constructing Visual Notations in Software Engineering (SLE 2008 SI)
  • T5H: Usability
    • It evaluates the cognitive effectiveness/usability of UML diagram notations from a human factors perspective.
  • T4F: Visualisation
    • Its subject is the visual syntax/notation of UML diagrams and how visual design principles affect comprehension.
  • T5E: Empirical Evaluation
    • It performs an evaluation-style analysis (grounded in cognitive/notation principles) to assess UML’s visual notation quality.
