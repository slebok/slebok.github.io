Formalization and Rule-Based Transformation of EMF Ecore-Based Models

Bernhard Schätz

  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-642-00434-6_15
  • T4B: Horizontal Transformation
    • It formalizes and applies rule-based transformations over EMF Ecore-based models, i.e., model-to-model rewriting.
  • T5D: Formal Methods
    • The paper emphasizes a formalization of the transformation framework and rules for rigor.
  • T3B: Transformation Languages
    • The rule-based transformation approach effectively defines a transformation language/notation for Ecore models.
