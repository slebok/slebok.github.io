SLE 2008 paper:
A Case Study in Grammar Engineering

Tiago L. Alves, Joost Visser

  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-642-00434-6_18
  • T6A: Experience Reports
    • As a ‘pearls’ case study, it reports experience and lessons learned from real grammar-engineering work.
  • T2B: Evolution
    • Grammar engineering is treated as an evolving artifact, and the case study focuses on how it is maintained/changed.
  • T5C: Testing
    • It typically stresses validation/testing practices for grammars as engineered artifacts.
