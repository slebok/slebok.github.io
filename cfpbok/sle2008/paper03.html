SLE 2008 paper:
Analyzing Rule-Based Behavioral Semantics of Visual Modeling Languages with Maude

José Eduardo Rivera, Esther Guerra, Juan de Lara, Antonio Vallecillo

  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-642-00434-6_5
  • T1C: Behavioural Semantics
    • It focuses on defining and analyzing executable/behavioral semantics of visual modeling languages using rewriting logic.
  • T5D: Formal Methods
    • It uses a formal-methods setting (Maude/rewrite rules) to enable rigorous semantic analysis.
  • T4D: Interpretation
    • It relies on executing/interpreting the semantic rules to explore and analyze modeled behavior.
