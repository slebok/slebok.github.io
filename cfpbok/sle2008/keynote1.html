SLE 2008 keynote:
The Field of Software Language Engineering

Anneke Kleppe

  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-642-00434-6_1
  • T6D: Synergies
    • This keynote frames SLE as an intersection of software engineering, programming languages, and modeling, emphasizing cross-community transfer of techniques.
  • T1A: Design
    • It discusses core concerns in designing software languages, including choices around notations and abstractions.
  • T4A: Workbenches
    • It highlights the role of language workbenches and tool support as a defining part of the SLE field.
