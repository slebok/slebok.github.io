SLE 2008 paper:
Towards an Incremental Update Approach for Concrete Textual Syntaxes for UUID-Based Model Repositories

Thomas Goldschmidt

  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-642-00434-6_11
  • T2B: Evolution
    • It proposes incremental updating to keep models and concrete textual syntaxes in sync during ongoing edits/maintenance.
  • T4A: Workbenches
    • It discusses (and motivates) tooling/framework support for incremental parsing and model updates.
  • T1D: Composition
    • The core challenge is integration between textual concrete syntax and UUID-based model repositories.
