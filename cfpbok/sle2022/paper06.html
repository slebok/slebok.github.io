SLE 2022 paper:
Selective Traceability for Rule-Based Model-to-Model Transformations

Qurat Ul Ain Ali, Dimitrios S. Kolovos, Konstantinos Barmpis

  • DOI: 10.1145/3567512.3567521
  • T5G: Traceability
    • The central topic is selective traceability for model-to-model transformations.
  • T3B: Transformation Languages
    • It targets rule-based model transformation languages and their execution behavior.
  • T4B: Horizontal Transformation
    • Trace models act as mappings between source and target models in horizontal transformations.
  • T5F: Performance
    • Selective traceability is motivated by scalability and performance of transformations.
