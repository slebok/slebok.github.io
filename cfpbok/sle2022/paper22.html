SLE 2022 paper:
Neural Language Models and Few Shot Learning for Systematic Requirements Processing in MDSE

Vincent Bertram, Miriam Boß, Evgeny Kusmenko, Imke Helene Nachmann, Bernhard Rumpe, Danilo Trotta, Louis Wachtmeister

  • DOI: 10.1145/3567512.3567534
  • T4G: AI-for-SLE
    • The paper applies large language models and few-shot learning to SLE problems (requirements to DSL).
  • T5A: Requirements
    • Its concrete task is processing and structuring natural-language requirements.
  • T3C: DSLs
    • It translates requirements into a structured DSL form and studies DSL-based formulations.
  • T5E: Empirical Evaluation
    • It reports an experimental evaluation of translation quality under different few-shot settings.
