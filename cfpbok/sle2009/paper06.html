SLE 2009 paper:
Multi-view Composition Language for Software Product Line Requirements

Mauricio Alférez, Jõao Santos, Ana Moreira, Alessandro Fabricio Garcia, Uirá Kulesza, João Araújo, Vasco Amaral

  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-642-12107-4_8
  • T5A: Requirements
    • Primary focus is a language for composing and managing software product line requirements across multiple views.
  • T2C: Variability
    • The setting is product lines/variability, which is a secondary but central context.
  • T1D: Composition
    • It emphasizes composition across views, i.e., integrating multiple requirement representations.
