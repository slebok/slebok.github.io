SLE 2009 paper:
Language Evolution in Practice: The History of GMF

Markus Herrmannsdörfer, Daniel Ratiu, Guido Wachsmuth

  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-642-12107-4_3
  • T2B: Evolution
    • The paper is explicitly about how the GMF language/tooling evolved over time and what that evolution looked like in practice.
  • T4A: Workbenches
    • GMF is a language workbench/tooling platform, so the narrative is grounded in evolution of meta-tools.
  • T2A: Lifecycle
    • It also concerns lifecycle aspects of a language framework (changes, migration, and long-term sustainability).
