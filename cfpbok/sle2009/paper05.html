SLE 2009 paper:
VML* – A Family of Languages for Variability Management in Software Product Lines

Steffen Zschaler, Pablo Sánchez, Jõao Santos, Mauricio Alférez, Awais Rashid, Lidia Fuentes, Ana Moreira, João Araújo, Uirá Kulesza

  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-642-12107-4_7
  • T2C: Variability
    • The paper proposes a family of languages dedicated to variability management in product lines.
  • T3C: DSLs
    • It introduces DSLs (a family thereof) for expressing variability concepts.
  • T1A: Design
    • It is about designing the constructs and structure of the VML* language family.
