SLE 2009 paper:
Ontological Metamodeling with Explicit Instantiation

Alfons Laarman, Ivan Kurtev

  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-642-12107-4_14
  • T3E: Ontologies
    • It derives a metalanguage from formal ontology and uses ontological notions (instantiation/generalization) as first-class concepts.
  • T3A: Meta-languages
    • The work proposes a new metalanguage (OGML) for metamodeling beyond abstract syntax.
  • T5D: Formal Methods
    • It argues for more precise conceptual/formal foundations in metamodeling, grounded in ontology theory.
