SLE 2009 paper:
Domain-Specific Metamodelling Languages for Software Language Engineering

Steffen Zschaler, Dimitrios S. Kolovos, Nikolaos Drivalos, Richard F. Paige, Awais Rashid

  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-642-12107-4_23
  • T3A: Meta-languages
    • It proposes/analyses metamodelling languages tailored to defining and engineering other languages.
  • T4A: Workbenches
    • Such metamodelling languages are typically realized as workbench capabilities and supporting tools.
  • T3C: DSLs
    • The goal is to better engineer domain-specific languages via dedicated metamodelling support.
