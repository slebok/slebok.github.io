SLE 2011 paper:
Semantics First! Rethinking the Language Design Process

Martin Erwig, Eric Walkingshaw

  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-642-28830-2_14
  • T1A: Design
    • It proposes a language design methodology that starts from semantics and derives other aspects.
  • T1C: Behavioural Semantics
    • The ‘semantics first’ stance makes behavioral/executable semantics a primary design driver.
  • T5D: Formal Methods
    • The approach relies on formal semantic reasoning to guide language design decisions.
