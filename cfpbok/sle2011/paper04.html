SLE 2011 paper:
Uniform Modularization of Workflow Concerns Using Unify

Niels Joncheere, Ragnhild Van Der Straeten

  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-642-28830-2_5
  • T1D: Composition
    • The main focus is modularization and composition of crosscutting workflow concerns.
  • T1E: Reuse
    • It aims to enable reuse of workflow concern modules across workflow models.
  • T4B: Horizontal Transformation
    • The approach targets applying concerns across different workflow notations/models, requiring model-to-model adaptation.
