SLE 2011 paper:
Metacompiling OWL Ontologies

Anders Nilsson, Görel Hedin

  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-642-28830-2_20
  • T3E: Ontologies
    • The paper directly targets OWL ontologies as the primary language artifact.
  • T4C: Vertical Transformation
    • ‘Metacompiling’ indicates a generative/compilation-style transformation from ontologies to executable artifacts.
  • T3B: Transformation Languages
    • The work centers on transformations over ontology descriptions to derive other representations.
