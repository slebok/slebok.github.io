SLE 2024 paper:
Aconite: Towards Generating Sirius-Based Graphical Editors from Annotated Metamodels

Nathan Richardson, Dimitrios S. Kolovos, Antonio García-Domínguez

  • DOI: 10.1145/3687997.3695642
  • T4A: Workbenches
    • It presents tooling to generate Sirius-based graphical editors automatically from annotated metamodels.
  • T4C: Vertical Transformation
    • Editor generation is a vertical/generative process that derives concrete tooling artifacts from language models.
  • T4F: Visualisation
    • The generated artifacts are graphical/diagrammatic editors, directly targeting visualization of DSL instances.
  • T3C: DSLs
    • The approach targets metamodel-based DSLs whose editors are derived from their language definitions.
