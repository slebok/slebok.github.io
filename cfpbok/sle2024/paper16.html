SLE 2024 paper:
Trellis: A Domain-Specific Language for Hidden Markov Models with Sparse Transitions

Lars Hummelgren, Viktor Palmkvist, Linnea Stjerna, Xuechun Xu, Joakim Jaldén, David Broman

  • DOI: 10.1145/3687997.3695641
  • T3C: DSLs
    • Trellis is a DSL for specifying sparse HMMs at a high level while generating efficient implementations.
  • T4C: Vertical Transformation
    • It relies on compilation/generation to GPU-accelerated code to achieve high performance.
  • T5F: Performance
    • A key goal is performance for large sparse models via GPU acceleration and sparsity-aware execution.
