SLE 2024 paper:
Towards an In-Context LLM-Based Approach for Automating the Definition of Model Views

James Pontes Miranda, Hugo Brunelière, Massimo Tisi, Gerson Sunyé

  • DOI: 10.1145/3687997.3695650
  • T4G: AI-for-SLE
    • The paper uses LLM-based assistance to automatically derive model-view definitions from context and examples.
  • T4B: Horizontal Transformation
    • Model views are constructed by mapping/transforming elements across heterogeneous metamodels and models.
  • T3C: DSLs
    • The problem is formulated over modeling languages and their view-definition DSLs.
  • T5E: Empirical Evaluation
    • The approach is validated by comparing generated view definitions against manually written ones.
