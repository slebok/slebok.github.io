SLE 2014 paper:
A SAT-Based Debugging Tool for State Machines and Sequence Diagrams

Petra Radeljak Kaufmann, Martin Kronegger, Andreas Pfandler, Martina Seidl, Magdalena Widl

  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-319-11245-9_2
  • Original shorter version of Intra- and interdiagram consistency checking of behavioral multiview models (SLE 2014 SI)
  • T5D: Formal Methods
    • It encodes consistency/debugging of behavioral models as a SAT problem to find witnesses/counterexamples.
  • T4A: Workbenches
    • The contribution includes an implemented debugging tool integrated with EMF-based modeling.
  • T1C: Behavioural Semantics
    • It reasons about the executable behavior of state machines and their interaction scenarios.
  • T5C: Testing
    • It employs grammar-based whitebox fuzzing as part of the evaluation/testing of the approach.
