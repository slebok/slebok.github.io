SLE 2014 paper:
Model Checking of CTL-Extended OCL Specifications

Robert Bill, Sebastian Gabmeyer, Petra Radeljak Kaufmann, Martina Seidl

  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-319-11245-9_13
  • T5D: Formal Methods
    • It extends OCL with temporal operators and performs model checking of the resulting specifications.
  • T1A: Design
    • A key contribution is the design of a CTL-extended constraint language integrated with modeling.
  • T4A: Workbenches
    • It provides tool support to translate/check such specifications against models.
