SLE 2014 paper:
Towards User-Friendly Projectional Editors

Markus Völter, Janet Siegmund, Thorsten Berger, Bernd Kolb

  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-319-11245-9_3
  • T4A: Workbenches
    • It focuses on language workbench/editor technology for projectional editing.
  • T1D: Composition
    • A major motivation is enabling language extension and composition through editor support.
  • T5H: Usability
    • It explicitly targets user-friendliness and evaluates/argues about usability issues in projectional editors.
