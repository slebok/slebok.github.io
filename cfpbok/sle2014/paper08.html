SLE 2014 paper:
A Metamodel Family for Role-Based Modeling and Programming Languages

Thomas Kühn, Max Leuthäuser, Sebastian Götz, Christoph Seidl, Uwe Aßmann

  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-319-11245-9_8
  • T1A: Design
    • It designs a family of language metamodels capturing role-based modeling/programming concepts.
  • T3A: Meta-languages
    • The work is grounded in metamodeling as a metalanguage for specifying families of related languages.
  • T1D: Composition
    • It relates modeling and programming perspectives, supporting integration across technical spaces.
  • T1B: Static Semantics
    • It characterizes constraints/properties of roles (e.g., well-formedness) that amount to static semantics.
