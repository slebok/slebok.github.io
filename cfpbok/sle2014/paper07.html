SLE 2014 paper:
Evaluating the Usability of a Visual Feature Modeling Notation

Aleksandar Jakšic, Robert B. France, Philippe Collet, Sudipto Ghosh

  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-319-11245-9_7
  • T5H: Usability
    • The paper’s main contribution is a usability-focused enhancement and assessment of feature-model notations.
  • T4F: Visualisation
    • It introduces/uses visualization (feature diagram rendering) to improve understanding of feature models.
  • T2C: Variability
    • Feature modeling is a central technique for product lines and variability management.
  • T5E: Empirical Evaluation
    • It reports an evaluation study to assess the impact of the proposed notation/tool support.
