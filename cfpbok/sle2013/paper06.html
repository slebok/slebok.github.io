SLE 2013 paper:
Micropatterns in Grammars

Vadim Zaytsev

  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-319-02654-1_7
  • T5B: Language Description Analysis
    • The paper introduces and studies “micropatterns” to analyze and comprehend grammar-like language descriptions (parsers, metamodels, data models).
  • T3A: Meta-languages
    • It treats grammars and related schema formalisms as meta-languages whose structure can be characterized and compared.
  • T2B: Evolution
    • Identifying recurring structural fragments supports grammar comprehension and can inform maintenance/evolution of grammarware artifacts.
