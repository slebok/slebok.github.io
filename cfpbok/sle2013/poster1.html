SLE 2013 poster:
Guided Grammar Convergence

Vadim Zaytsev

  • DOI: 10.48550/arXiv.1503.08476
  • T5B: Language Description Analysis
    • The main contribution is analysis and reconciliation of language descriptions (grammars) via milestones of identity/equivalence and structural matching.
  • T4B: Horizontal Transformation
    • It aims to infer (bidirectional) transformation steps between different grammar representations, i.e., mappings/conversions across formalisms.
  • T3A: Meta-languages
    • The entire setting is about meta-languages for syntax/grammar specification (grammars “in a broad sense”), including normalization to a normal form.
  • T3B: Transformation Languages
    • Conventional and guided convergence are explicitly framed as sequences of grammar transformation steps (including automatically inferred ones).
