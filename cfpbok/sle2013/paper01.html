SLE 2013 paper:
Developing a Domain-Specific Language for Scheduling in the European Energy Sector

Stefan Sobernig, Mark Strembeck, Andreas J. Beck

  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-319-02654-1_2
  • T3C: DSLs
    • The central contribution is the design and implementation of a domain-specific language for expressing electricity trading schedules.
  • T4B: Horizontal Transformation
    • A key motivation is to cope with multiple proprietary/standard schedule formats, which calls for mapping/conversion across representations.
  • T6B: Industrial
    • The work is grounded in an industrial domain (European energy trading) and reports on a real development project.
