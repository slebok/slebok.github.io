SLE 2013 poster:
Towards Controlling Refinements of Statecharts

Conner Hansen, Eugene Syriani, Lucio Levi

  • DOI: 10.48550/arXiv.1503.07266
  • T2B: Evolution
    • The paper’s core problem is controlling incremental refinement across versions of a Statecharts model to support predictable evolution and maintenance.
  • T5D: Formal Methods
    • It defines a formal refinement relation and constraints (e.g., inclusion conditions over events/guards/structure) to guarantee behaviour preservation.
  • T4C: Vertical Transformation
    • “Refinement” is treated as a disciplined stepwise development relation (additive, structure-preserving), i.e., a vertical refinement discipline over models.
  • T3C: DSLs
    • Statecharts are treated as a domain-specific modeling language whose refinement rules constrain how models can be extended.
