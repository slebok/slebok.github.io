SLE 2013 paper:
Micro-Machinations: A DSL for Game Economies

Paul Klint, Riemer van Rozen

  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-319-02654-1_3
  • T3C: DSLs
    • The paper proposes a dedicated DSL to let game designers specify and communicate game-economy mechanics.
  • T4E: Simulation
    • The DSL targets the modeling and (typically) simulation/analysis of game economies to understand emergent behavior.
  • T5H: Usability
    • A stated goal is improving designer productivity and designer–engineer communication, i.e., human-centered usability concerns.
