SLE 2013 paper:
xMOF: Executable DSMLs Based on fUML

Tanja Mayerhofer, Philip Langer, Manuel Wimmer, Gerti Kappel

  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-319-02654-1_4
  • T1C: Behavioural Semantics
    • The main focus is defining and supporting behavioral/executable semantics for DSMLs by building on fUML.
  • T4D: Interpretation
    • It aims at executing models (incl. debugging/simulation support), i.e., interpretation/runtime facilities for DSMLs.
  • T3C: DSLs
    • The contribution targets domain-specific modeling languages and how to make them executable.
