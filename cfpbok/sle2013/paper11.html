SLE 2013 paper:
A Model-Driven Approach to Enhance Tool Interoperability Using the Theory of Models of Computation

Papa Issa Diallo, Joël Champeau, Loïc Lagadec

  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-319-02654-1_12
  • T4B: Horizontal Transformation
    • The core problem is interoperability across heterogeneous tools, addressed through explicit mappings/exchanges between executable models.
  • T5D: Formal Methods
    • It leverages formal “models of computation” to reason about and constrain executable model exchanges in a principled way.
  • T1D: Composition
    • The paper is fundamentally about coordinating and integrating heterogeneous tool chains and execution models.
