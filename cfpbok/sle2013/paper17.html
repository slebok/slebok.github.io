SLE 2013 paper:
Mapping-Aware Megamodeling: Design Patterns and Laws

Zinovy Diskin, Sahar Kokaly, Tom Maibaum

  • DOI: 10.1007/978-3-319-02654-1_18
  • T3A: Meta-languages
    • Megamodels act as meta-level descriptions of models and mappings; the paper proposes richer, mapping-aware megamodeling constructs and principles.
  • T4B: Horizontal Transformation
    • The work centers on explicit mappings between models (transformations/conversions) and how they are represented and reasoned about.
  • T5D: Formal Methods
    • It formulates “laws” and design patterns for megamodels, emphasizing principled (often formal) constraints on mapping structures and operations.
