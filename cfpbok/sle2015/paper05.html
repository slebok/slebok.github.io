SLE 2015 paper:
Example-based validation of domain-specific visual languages

Jesús J. López-Fernández, Esther Guerra, Juan de Lara

  • DOI: 10.1145/2814251.2814256
  • T5C: Testing
    • The main focus is validating DSL definitions by checking them against example instances (testing/validation).
  • T3C: DSLs
    • It targets domain-specific languages, specifically domain-specific *visual* languages.
  • T4F: Visualisation
    • Because the DSLs are visual, the work directly involves graphical notation and its interpretation.
  • T4A: Workbenches
    • The approach is motivated by and discussed in the context of tool support for DSL engineering.
