SLE 2015 paper:
On integrating graphical and textual editors for a UML profile based domain specific language: an industrial experience

Salome Maro, Jan-Philipp Steghöfer, Anthony Anjorin, Matthias Tichy, Lars Gelin

  • DOI: 10.1145/2814251.2814253
  • T4A: Workbenches
    • The main contribution is tool/editor support that integrates graphical and textual notations for a DSL.
  • T4F: Visualisation
    • It directly concerns graphical notation and its synchronization with textual views (visualisation/editing).
  • T3C: DSLs
    • The case revolves around a UML-profile-based domain-specific language.
  • T6B: Industrial
    • The study is positioned as an industrial experience report with an industrial partner context.
